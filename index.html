The Legend of Lo Shu

According to Chinese legend, around 4000 years ago, Emperor Yu saw a sacred turtle emerge from the flooded Luo River with a magic 3x3 pattern of dot-clusters on its shell.

The dots represented numbers 1 to 9, arranged so that every row, column, and diagonal summed to exactly 15 — the length of each of the 24 cycles of the Chinese solar calendar. This grid became the foundation of Feng Shui and cosmic numerology.
